How a new leader is added

One structured profile becomes nodes and relationships

Professional identity

  • Name and concise professional title.
  • A 50–80 word biography.
  • City and active markets.

Capabilities and evidence

  • Primary sectors and expertise.
  • Prior work and measurable outcomes.
  • Assets, channels, and available relationships.

Offer and ask

  • What can the member provide?
  • Which opportunities are they seeking?
  • What can be executed within 14 days?
