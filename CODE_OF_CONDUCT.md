# Community Code of Conduct

Delta Business Hub is committed to a professional, respectful, and inclusive collaboration environment.

Participants are expected to:

- Communicate constructively and distinguish facts from assumptions.
- Respect intellectual property, privacy, and confidentiality.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and deceptive conduct.
- Disclose relevant conflicts of interest.
- Avoid unsolicited promotion that does not create clear community value.
- Honor agreed responsibilities or communicate changes promptly.

Unacceptable behavior may result in content removal, restricted participation, or referral to the relevant platform provider.

Report conduct concerns through a repository issue only when the information is safe to make public. Security or sensitive concerns should follow `SECURITY.md`.
